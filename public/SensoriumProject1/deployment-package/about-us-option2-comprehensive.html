What is Sensorium?

The Biology

In biology, the sensorium is where all five senses come together to create one complete perception of the world. Your eyes see color, your ears hear sound, your fingers feel texture—but it's only when these senses combine that you truly understand what you're experiencing.

The Metaphor

Each clinical research site has its own local culture, shaped by the community it serves. One site may excel at building trust in a Hispanic neighborhood. Another may understand urban and rural perspectives. Together, they create complete understanding across real-world populations.

Like the five senses, each site provides a unique perspective. Together, they create insights that no single site could capture alone. That's better science.

The Sensorium Effect

Our blueprint for better science: sophisticated operational backbone while protecting the local culture of each site.

What Sites Gain

  • Centralized business development and study acquisition
  • Finance, HR, payroll, and compliance support
  • Technology systems (CTMS, eReg, CRM)
  • Patient recruitment and marketing support
  • Regulatory operations and QA oversight

What Sites Keep

  • Your brand, name, and local reputation
  • Your team and site culture
  • Clinical autonomy and decision-making
  • Community relationships and patient trust
  • Operational freedom and local control

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To unite local communities and diverse patient voices into one cohesive system—where each site retains its independence, culture, and community focus, while gaining the infrastructure and support to thrive at scale. Where diversity isn't just represented, it's built into the science itself.

Our Vision

A future where the clinical research industry doesn't feel like a factory. Where operational strength and community soul coexist. Where sites can scale without sacrifice, and diversity becomes a scientific advantage rather than a compliance checkbox.

Matt Kiernan

Founder & CEO

Sensorium Clinical Research

Matt Kiernan is a life sciences entrepreneur who believes culture is the foundation of great companies. Over the past two decades, he has built and exited two successful organizations in the pharmaceutical services industry—Pharmica Consulting and Intrinsic Clinical Systems—both acquired by ProPharma Group in 2021.

Matt's approach blends operational rigor with cultural intention. At Pharmica, he launched a company-wide program built around the philosophy "Work hard, have fun, be great," inspiring consultants, clients, and partners to uphold a standard of mutual respect and service. At Intrinsic, he applied that same mindset to technology, creating tools that helped clinical teams work smarter and stay connected.

Today, he brings that philosophy to Sensorium Clinical Research—building a network where investigators, patients, and staff feel supported and valued.

Education & Credentials: MBA in Finance from Villanova University, dual undergraduate degrees in Business and Psychology from the University of Pittsburgh, certifications in Project Management and Six Sigma Black Belt.
