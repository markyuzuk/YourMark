How Sensorium Clinical Trials Work

A simple guide to understanding your journey from start to finish

Contact Us

Call, email, or visit a local site

Screening

See if the study is right for you

Participate

Receive treatment & monitoring

1

Initial Contact & Screening

When you reach out, we'll have a friendly conversation about your health history and the study requirements. This helps us determine if the trial might be a good fit for you.

What We'll Ask:

  • • Your medical history
  • • Current medications
  • • Your availability

What You'll Learn:

  • • Study purpose & goals
  • • Time commitment
  • • Potential benefits & risks
2

Informed Consent Process

Before you join, we'll walk you through every detail of the study. You'll receive a consent form that explains everything in plain language. Take your time, ask questions, and only sign when you're completely comfortable.

3

Active Participation

During the study, you'll visit the research site regularly. Each visit typically includes health checks, treatment administration, and monitoring. Your research team will track your progress and answer any questions.

Regular Visits

Scheduled appointments for treatment & monitoring

Health Checks

Vital signs, lab work, and assessments

Support Team

Doctors, nurses & Patient Concierge

4

Follow-Up & Results

Your journey doesn't end when the study does. We stay connected to make sure you're doing well and keep you informed about what your participation helped achieve.

What Happens Next:

  • • We check in to make sure you're healthy and feeling good
  • • You'll learn about the study findings and what they mean for patients like you
  • • Your contribution helps shape the future of medicine for your community
