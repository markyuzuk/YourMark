Your Journey in Clinical Research

Every major medical breakthrough began with someone like you. At Sensorium, you're not just a participant—you're a partner in advancing medicine while receiving compassionate, expert care.

Why Participate in Clinical Research?

Clinical trials are essential to advancing medicine and discovering new treatments. By participating, you contribute to medical research that may help future generations while potentially accessing cutting-edge treatments.

Access to New Treatments

Gain access to innovative therapies before they're widely available. You may receive cutting-edge treatments that could make a real difference in your health.

Expert Medical Care

Receive close monitoring and care from experienced medical professionals throughout your participation. Your health and safety are our top priorities.

No Cost Treatment

Study-related care and medications are typically provided at no cost to participants. Many studies also provide compensation for your time and travel.

Your Journey with Sensorium

From your first inquiry to study completion, we're with you every step of the way.

1

Initial Contact & Screening

Reach out to us through our website, phone, or your local Sensorium site. We'll discuss your health history, current conditions, and what studies might be a good fit for you.

What to expect: A friendly conversation about your health, no pressure, and all your questions answered.

2

Informed Consent

Before you join any study, we'll walk you through every detail: what the study involves, potential risks and benefits, time commitment, and your rights as a participant. You'll have plenty of time to ask questions and discuss with family.

Remember: Participation is 100% voluntary. You can withdraw at any time, for any reason.

3

Study Participation

During the study, you'll visit your local Sensorium site for regular check-ups, treatments, and monitoring. Our research team will track your progress closely and ensure you're comfortable throughout.

Your support team: Licensed local physician, dedicated research coordinator, and your Patient Concierge.

4

Follow-Up & Completion

After the study ends, we'll continue to monitor your health and share results with you. You'll always have access to your medical records and can stay connected with our team.

What happens next: Study results are shared, and you'll know the impact of your contribution to medical science.

Your Rights as a Participant

Every clinical trial is designed with patient safety as the top priority. Before joining a study, you'll receive detailed information about what's involved, and you can ask questions at any time.

"At Sensorium sites, you're treated as a partner in progress, with compassionate care throughout your participation."

  • You have the right to know all details about the study before deciding to participate
  • Participation is completely voluntary—you can withdraw at any time, for any reason
  • Your privacy and medical information will be protected under strict guidelines
  • You'll be compensated for your time and travel expenses
  • All questions will be answered by qualified medical staff
  • All studies are conducted under strict FDA and IRB guidelines
