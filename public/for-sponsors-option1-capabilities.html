The Patient Concierge Model

Every Sensorium site features a Patient Concierge—a local advocate whose only job is to guide, support, and listen to patients from day one through study completion.

Guides patients through informed consent with clear explanations
Coordinates visit schedules and sends reminders
Serves as primary point of contact between visits
Addresses concerns before they become reasons to withdraw
